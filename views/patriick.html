Patrick Ssenjovu

Patrick Ssenjovu has come a long way to become a successful actor in Uganda as well as on the global platform. With self drive and determination to explore his passion Patrick managed to scale up to bag award winning roles both locally and internationally. Some of the projects he has worked on include ‘Game 6’, ‘The Interpreter’, ‘X-Men’ Origins: Wolverine’, ‘Baby Wont You Please Come Home’ among others. Worth noting is that Patrick is also a film producer and director and he is best known for his short film ‘Awaken’.
