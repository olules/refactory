Cleopatra Koheirwe

Cleopatra Koheirwe is a well known actress in Uganda. She is also a writer, media personality and a singer. As an actress Cleopatra got the opportunity to do her first onscreen role in the famous film ‘The Last King of Scotland’ in which she played the role of Joy. Her role in the film boosted her acting career as she has been able to garner more acting roles. Some of the Film and Tv projects she has worked on include ‘Sense 8’, ‘Yogera’, ’27 Guns’ among others. Cleopatra continues to excel in her acting career alongside her other titles.
